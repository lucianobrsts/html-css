Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and dish, perfect for any meal. This classic Omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled width your choice, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation:5 minutes
  • Cooking:5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Paper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter of oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meats, herbs
